Our Story

Made with purpose,
worn with pride.

BEAST Fashions started in a small Jaipur studio in 2013, with one goal: make clothes that feel as good as they look. We were tired of fast fashion that fell apart after three washes — so we went back to craft.

We work directly with artisan weavers and ethical factories across Rajasthan and Tamil Nadu. Every fabric is chosen for durability, comfort, and low impact on the environment. No greenwashing — just honest clothing.

  • Ethical production from fibre to finish
  • Climate-neutral shipping since 2021
  • 1% of revenue to textile worker welfare funds
